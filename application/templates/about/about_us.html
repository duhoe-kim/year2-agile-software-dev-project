About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of students, who started this ingredient recommendation web application, named Nutrition Wisdoms, in hopes to reduce the struggles for people who have no idea what to eat and how much to intake.


How Do We Help

We hope to provide ingredient recommendations for users who wish to manage their struggles to prepare meals, weight and health based on The Healthy Plate. We have explored about the measures to determine how weight could be managed and studied about how diets are classified.


Who Do We Target

We target potential users, aged 18 years old and above, who often cook meals at home and struggle with what they can prepare for their meals due to various factors.

People, who are interested in cooking more meals at home in the hopes to manage their weight, can seek suggestions on our Nutrition Wisdoms web application. They will be able to manage their calorie intake and track their weight change.


Our Scope / Disclaimer

As Nutrition Wisdoms is made to focus on recommending ingredients mainly based on their body mass index as its unique main feature, we would like to target our general community and aim to include those who have straightforward personal considerations which are common in our community.

We will also make sure to comply with Singapore’s Personal Data Protection Act to utilise users’ data solely for the benefit of providing better recommendations to our potential users.

However, we will limit our scope within Singapore in the consideration to delegate more time for the development of our web application. In addition, we will highlight to the users that Nutrition Wisdoms is not a website that contains every possible personal considerations and take our recommendations as suggestions but not a substitute of healthcare professionals.
